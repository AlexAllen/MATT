# Overview
## Summary

As a first go, I am going to leave out the wifi module, but include most of the other functionality, using sensors that I either already have, use elsewhere, or come recommended on TRF. 

## Major components

1x Teensy 4.1 with flash and psram 
1x Hope RF HM-TRP-868 RF module
	- Connect with UART
1x BMP 390
	- Connect with SPI
1x LSM6DSV80X
	- Connect with SPI
1x LIS2MDL
	- Connect with I2C
1x Quectel L80-R
	- Connect with UART
2x VN5E160STR-E for deployment charges
1x buzzer
4x pwm out
1x Pololu D30V30MAL
